Core Features

Salon Owner Stylist

Everything the floor demands. Nothing extra.

Built for owners who work between clients — fast to open, obvious to use, revenue-focused.

Smart Appointment Booking

Day-timeline schedule, owner booking with conflict prevention, public client self-booking link, and automated email confirmations — no double-bookings, fewer no-shows. Live in v1.

Stylist Management

Assign stylists by skill and availability. Track schedules, specialties, and performance. Every chair productive, every stylist matched to the right client.

Product Inventory Tracking

Real-time stock levels for every product. Low-stock alerts before you run out. Usage tracked per service so you know exactly what you need and when.

Client Retention

Automated post-visit follow-ups and loyalty rewards. Surfaces lapsed clients. Visit history keeps every stylist briefed before the client sits down.
