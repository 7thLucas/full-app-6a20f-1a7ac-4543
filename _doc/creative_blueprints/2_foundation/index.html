Fragmented salon operations

The Problem

Beauty salon owners juggle multiple systems to manage appointments, assign stylists, track inventory, and follow up with clients. Overbooking, underutilized stylists, stockouts, and forgotten follow-ups erode profitability and customer loyalty.

The Solution

Beauty salon owners struggle with manual appointment scheduling, disorganized stylist assignments, scattered inventory tracking, and missed follow-ups—leading to double bookings, low utilization rates, and lost repeat customers.

Target Audience

Independent and multi-location beauty salon owners with 2–20 stylists

Personal Motivation

Reclaim time spent on admin, increase chair utilization, and build lasting client relationships through seamless experience
