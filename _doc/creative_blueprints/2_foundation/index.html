The Foundation

Salons run on chaos.
Revenue escapes through the gaps.

The Problem

Salon owners coordinate appointments over WhatsApp, track inventory on paper, and manage stylists from memory. The result: double-bookings, no-shows with no warning, inventory running dry mid-appointment, and clients who drift away because no one followed up.

The Solution

GlowDesk gives hands-on salon owners — often stylists themselves — one calm mobile app: real-time availability, client self-booking, automated reminders, stylist management, inventory alerts, and built-in retention tools. Replace the chaos without changing how salons think.
